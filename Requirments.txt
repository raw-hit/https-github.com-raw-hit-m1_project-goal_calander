- This mini project  is a console application without graphics. 

- Many windows properties have been used in this project. 
- Besides the color used in backgrounds, the days of the month are white and the vacations (Sundays) 
  are indicated using the red foreground color. 

- In this mini project,
  find out the day corresponding to a given date and view the days and dates
  corresponding to a particular month+year. 

   
- compiled in Code::Blocks IDE with GCC compiler.
